Key Differences

  • A black hole is a region in space with extreme gravity, while an exoplanet is a planet outside our solar system.
  • Black holes are formed from collapsing stars, but exoplanets form around stars or freely float in space.

Interesting Facts

  1. Black holes consume everything that comes near them, while exoplanets may support life.
